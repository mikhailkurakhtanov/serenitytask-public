I. First step

Hello! I see you can't wait to start using the app and take control of your time and tasks? Excellent! This user guide will help you get familiar with all the features of SerenitaTask in order to get the most out of the application.

Create an account

Before you can start using the application, you need to create an account. To do this, click on the "Plant" button. That's right, the button to go to the account creation form is exactly what it is called, because you will have to grow your own plant by your own efforts, but more on that later!

So, if you have a Google account, then click on the button with the Google logo and grant all the required permissions. In the future, you can unlink your account if necessary. In the case of manual registration, fill in all the required fields of the form and confirm the registration. An activation email will be sent to your email address immediately. Follow the link contained in the letter and cheers - now you are ready to log in to the system.

Authorization

To enter the system, click on the "Grow" button to return to the process of developing your indoor leafy friend. If you previously registered with a Google account or linked it in your account settings, this will greatly simplify your login into the system. If you do not want to log in to the system every time you open the application, select the "Remember me" option. Now you are ready to start using the application!

II. Plant Management

A plant is your progress visualization. It helps your track your effectivity and make a desicions to plan smart.

Create a plant

To create a plant click on the Plant a seed button. Then select a type of a plant that you want to grow. Plant leveling means how many leaves can this type of plant grow.

Track your progress

To see how your plant's experience change, click on the list icon below your plant.!
