Welcome to the

SerenityTask is an app that was built to help you achieve your goals and live meaningful life.

Features

Unique gaming experience

Grow a cute plant from scratch completing tasks and getting achievements. Complete tasks before deadline to make your plant grow and don't let it die. Each action can afford on your plant as the real life on your mental health

Be more disciplined and consistent

Break your procrastination working alone by joining focus sessions with your friends to discipline each other and form good study habits on the road to your goals

Time and deadlines tracking

Time is the most valuable thing in our life. See how much time do you afford on each task. Prioritize by smart way and don't let the deadlines expire

Flexible task management

Use your favorite task management apps' functions to store all necessary information for each task in one place. Planning, prioritizing, time tracking, notifications and more - in one place (also supports GoogleCalendar integration)
